# Skill: Product Acceptance Review

Use this skill for PM Agent review.

## Steps

1. Read the business goal and target user.
2. Review QA evidence first.
3. Walk the primary user journey.
4. Evaluate intuitiveness, copy, integration with app, accessibility, and edge cases.
5. Create PM feedback for confusing or low-value behavior.
6. Require design/dev fix for blocking product issues.
7. Pass only when the story is product-ready for human AI PM review.
