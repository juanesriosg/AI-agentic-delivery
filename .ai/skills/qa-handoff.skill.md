# Skill: QA Handoff

## Purpose

Give the manager, QA, or stakeholder enough evidence to test and accept the work without reading all code.

## Required content

- What changed in user/business terms.
- Acceptance criteria status.
- Test matrix.
- Automated validation results.
- Manual QA steps.
- Test data used.
- Known bugs or gaps.
- Risk level.
- Rollback notes.
- Areas for focused QA.
